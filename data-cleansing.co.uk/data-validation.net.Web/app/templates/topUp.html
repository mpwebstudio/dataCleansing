How it works

Choose the amount of credit you want to add and pay for your usage on a standart pay as you use model. We will automatically convert your top up amount into credits as follow:

- £25 pounds top up into 3 000 credits;
- £50 pounds top up into 7 200 credits;
- £100 pounds top up into 15 600 credits;
- £150 pounds top up into 25 800 credits;
- £200 pounds top up into 41 400 credits;
- £500 pounds top up into 126 000 credits;

All credit purchased can be used in a combination of any product for a period of 12 montths. If a new purchase has been made within this 12 months all old credits if any will be automatically add to your new credit balance.

We have a simple pricing system. You will be charget 6 credits for any one validation request or 1 credit per record for any cleansing request. For example if you submit a CSV file with 15 000 records to be cleansed tru our email cleansing system it will cost you 15 000 credits.
